About Us

We Are The Oldest School Building in Sindh

Founded in 1855, it is the oldest public school in Sindh; named after the prominent educationist ‘Narayan Jaganath Vaidya’ a legacy of 169 Years. The school was adopted by Akhuwat in 2015 and was completely transformed to nurture 1,400+ Free Thinking students via a 360-degree integrated model supporting Critical Thinking and Technology Adoption.

Vision

To inculcate in our fellows a knowledge base that cultivates: Free Thinkers, Creative Problem Solvers, and Compassionate Leaders who challenge the conventional wisdom of the world.
