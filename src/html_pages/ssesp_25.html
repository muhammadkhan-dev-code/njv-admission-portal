NJV Government Higher Secondary School, Karachi Scholarship Program for Grade 8

We are pleased to announce the scholarship program for Grade 8 at NJV Government Higher Secondary School, Karachi. The test is being conducted in collaboration with STS. Please follow the instructions below to apply and ensure that you meet all the requirements.

How to Apply

  • Click on “Apply Here” at the bottom of the page after reading all the instructions carefully.
  • Select Apply Online to access the admission form.
  • Fill in all required details accurately. Incorrect or incomplete forms will be rejected.
  • After completing the form, a challan with a unique ID will be generated.
  • Pay the challan fee at Sindh Bank, Faysal Bank, or UBL Bank.
  • Upload a scanned copy of the paid challan (STS copy) on the website.
  • Attach the required documents before submitting the form.
  • Double-check all information and uploads before final submission.

Required Documents

Applicants must prepare and upload clear scanned copies of the following documents along with the application form:

  1. Recent passport-size photograph
  2. Student’s Birth Certificate
  3. B-Form
  4. Father’s CNIC copy
  5. Domicile of Father / Legal Guardian
  6. General Register (GR) scan copy
  7. Original bank deposit slip (STS copy)
